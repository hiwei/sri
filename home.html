“There is nowhere like Sri Lanka.”

　A smiling sun all year round, golden beaches, ancient cities, exciting safaris, colourful culture and lovely people. Found at the tip of India, this little exotic island is abundant with marvelous discoveries for any tourist. Be it a journey of exploring the wilderness, an Ayurvedic wellness holiday, a relaxing get-away in the sun, there is something for everyone.
